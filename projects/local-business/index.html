Our Story

Crafting beauty since 2012.

At Glow, we believe that true elegance is a reflection of your inner self. Founded by Sarah Jenkins, our salon has been a sanctuary for those seeking premium care and personalized styling for over a decade.

Our philosophy is simple: we combine artistic vision with technical precision to elevate your natural beauty. We use only the finest sustainable products, ensuring that your experience is as kind to the earth as it is to your hair.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Within 24 hours, a fee may apply as we hold that time specifically for you.

Do you offer bridal consultations? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive bridal consultations and trial sessions to ensure your dream look is perfect on your big day.

What products do you use? +

We exclusively use salon-grade, sustainable, and organic products from Oway and Kevin Murphy to ensure the health of your hair.
