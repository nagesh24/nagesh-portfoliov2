Our Philosophy

Beyond Real Estate

Founded in 2001 in Geneva, LuxEstate was born from a singular vision: to create a private brokerage that treats real estate not as a transaction, but as art advisory.

We understand that our clients are acquiring more than square footage; they are securing legacies, sanctuaries, and masterpieces of architecture. Our discretion is absolute, and our network is unmatched.
