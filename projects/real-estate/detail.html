About This Property

Villa Serena represents the pinnacle of California coastal living. Perched above the Pacific with commanding 180° ocean views, this architectural masterpiece by Olson Kundig was conceived as a dialogue between the elemental landscape and refined domesticity.

The property features five en-suite bedrooms, each with floor-to-ceiling glazing, a chef's kitchen appointed with La Cornue range and Calacatta marble surfaces, a 65-foot infinity pool, private spa, and direct beach access via a funicular carved into the cliff face.
